Alaif: Ink Letter Slicer

Slice Arabic letters & ink

Calligraphy meets Fruit Ninja. Swipe to slice drifting Arabic letters on warm paper cards, chain combos, dodge ink bombs. Offline, no ads, no accounts.

About the game

Alaif is a fast, tactile letter-slicing arcade game inspired by Thuluth-style Arabic calligraphy. Letters drift up across warm, deckle-edged paper cards — swipe your finger to slice them with a satisfying ink-blade cut. Chain three or more letters in a single stroke for a gold combo, and watch out for ink bombs.

This is an arcade game, not a learning app — the letters are here for their shape and rhythm, not as a lesson.

Frequently asked questions

Does Alaif need an internet connection?

No. Alaif runs entirely on your device — no accounts, no sign-in, no ads, no in-app purchases, and no network connection of any kind.

Where is my high score stored?

Locally, on your phone only. Deleting the app deletes your high score; it is not synced or backed up anywhere else.

How do I turn off sound, music, or vibration?

Open Settings from the main menu or the pause screen. Sound effects, music, and haptics can each be toggled independently.

How do lives work?

You start with 3 lives. Letting a letter fall un-sliced costs a life, and so does slicing an ink bomb. The run ends when all three are gone.

I found a bug — what should I do?

Email the address below with your device model, iOS version, and a short description of what happened. Screenshots or screen recordings help a lot.

Privacy

Alaif collects no data. There are no analytics, no trackers, no advertising identifiers, and no network calls. Everything — including your best score and your settings — lives only on your device.
